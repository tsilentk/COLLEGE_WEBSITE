Masters Of Technology

  • M.Tech in Computer Science Engineering                              18 seats  

  • M.Tech in Digital Communication                                            18 seats  

  • M.Tech Thermal Engineering                                                   18 seats  



  1. Minimum Qualification:-

    •The candidates should have BE/B.Tech/MSc or equivalent degree in relevant course of engineering with not less than 60% marks or 6.5 CGPA in qualifying examination.
