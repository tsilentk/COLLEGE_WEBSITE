CIVIL ENGINEERING

Prof. Wasim Khan
HOD
Dept. of Civil Engineering

From the beginning of civilization this branch of Engineering provides the foundation for all types of construction work. It deals with traditional as well as innovative methods of engineering with safety measures and regular maintenance. Infrastructural development deals with planning, surveying, material testing, designing of structure, supervision, construction management and execution of works. The demand for civil engineers is constantly increasing and opportunities for entrepreneurship and employment, as well as for community and nation building are unlimited. Placements and scope in this field are increasing day by day, promising a bright future for civil engineers.

ELECTRICAL & ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

Prof. Anil Kumar Sahu
HOD
Dept. of Electrical & Electronics Engineering

The Electrical and Electronics branch of Engineering is of premier importance as there is not a single industry (small scale or large corporate sector) which does not require electrical equipment. This branch of engineering offers maximum job opportunities in various fields of electrical and electronics engineering, such as consultancy, design, manufacturing, testing, quality control, erection work , commissioning and maintenance. Apart from these specialized engineering jobs, the software and system engineering sector is always open for electrical and electronics engineering graduates who are also eligible for BANK SERVICES, IES, IPS, IAS, DEFENCE, DRDO, public sector jobs, PSC and many more. Our well-equipped labs provide students with the opportunity to gain practical and real-life experience by making projects under the guidance of expert faculty.

APPLIED PHYSICS

Dept. Of Applied Physics

Engineering Physics teaches the application of modern physics in accordance with new technological advances. These are the tools being used to develop advanced technologies in fields as diverse as:

  • Mechatronics
  • Nano-system engineering
  • Nuclear engineering
  • Sustainable energy systems
  • Optoelectronics
  • Semiconductor devices

HUMANITIES

Dept. Of Humanities

With the rapid growth of the industrial and tertiary sector, professional communication has acquired great importance. More and more organizations recruit engineers with good Communication Skills which play a pivotal role in all the three phases of the recruitment process i.e. the Written phase, G.D. and Personal Interview. The main aim of Communication Skills is to harness reading, writing, listening and speaking skills of students with special attention to English which is an international language. Role of the Department: The department of Humanities was established to teach English to engineering students. The English Language Lab is a vibrant hub through which our students are gaining confidence and expertise not only in communication skills but also in terms of developing their potential for social interaction and exchange latent qualities of initiative and leadership essential in this highly competitive corporate world.
Department Goals:-

  • • Train students to communicate effectively in English.
  • • Prepare them for group discussion and personal interviews.
  • • Guide them to present themselves with confidence as persons with positive traits-both ability and good manners-as assets to interactions with future employers.
